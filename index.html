ARC Project

Adaptive and Resilient Collaboration

About the Project

The ARC project explores new paradigms of physical and cognitive collaboration between humans and machines. It focuses on adaptive, resilient systems designed for interaction, deformation, and embodied intelligence, across various domains including healthcare, robotics, and design.
